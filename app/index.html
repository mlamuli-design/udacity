Who We Are

Coders Eswatini is an online learning platform that specializes in providing high-quality courses

Particularly in technology and business. It offers a unique educational model through its Nanodegree programs, which are designed to equip learners with practical skills needed for in-demand careers.

Courses We Offer

Artificial intelligence

Simulation of human intelligence in machines, enabling them to learn, reason, and solve problems autonomously using algorithms.

Data science

The field of analyzing large datasets using statistical methods, machine learning, and algorithms to extract insights and make predictions.

Programming

Process of writing code to develop software, automate tasks, solve problems, and control computer systems efficiently and logically.

Cloud computing

allows users to access computing resources like storage, servers, and applications over the internet.
